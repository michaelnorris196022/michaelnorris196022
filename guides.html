Build Roadmap

  1. Define your goal: gaming, content creation, workstation, or general use.
  2. Pick your CPU and platform (e.g., AMD Ryzen vs. Intel Core).
  3. Select a compatible motherboard (chipset, socket, and form factor).
  4. Choose GPU performance for your target resolution and refresh rate.
  5. Memory (RAM) capacity/speed; storage (SSD/NVMe) capacity.
  6. Case and cooling: airflow, radiator clearance, and noise level.
  7. Power supply (PSU) with adequate wattage and efficiency.
  8. Assemble, cable-manage, update BIOS, and install OS/drivers.
